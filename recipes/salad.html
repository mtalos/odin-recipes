Tuna salad

Description

A superb and tasty salad that you can't get enough of.

It combines fish, leafy green vegetables, beans, avocado, onions and on top of that it's finished with a delicious dijon vinaigrette.
My wife and I make this salad every two or three days and we love it.

Ingredients

  • a mix of greens like green salad, arugula, spinach, lettuce, etc.
  • 1 can of tuna (I use in water)
  • 1 avocado (cubed)
  • 1 can of red kidney beans
  • 1 small red onion cut in slices
  • salt, pepper, olive oil, pumpkin seeds
  • cheese (optional)
  • 2 slices of bread for making croutons (optional)
  • for the vinaigrette:
    • 3 parts olive oil
    • 1 part lemon juice
    • salt, pepper
    • 1 teaspoon of dijon mustard
    • a bit of apple cider vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the vinaigrette first: add all the ingredients in a jar and shake well. Add more oil/lemon juice if necessary and season to taste.
  2. Put the greens in a big bowl and pour the vinaigrette on top, mix well with your hands until combined well.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients: tuna, beans, avocado, onion, cheese (of your choice, I use cheddar cheese) and a handful of pumpkin seeds and mix well.
  4. If you want a bit of crunchiness, toast the bread in a pan with olive oil then cut it in small bite-sized pieces and add to the salad.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
